Google Sheet to `zosinfo-data.json` Converter

Instructions

  1. Open your Google Sheet.
  2. In the menu, go to File > Share > Publish to web.
  3. In the dialog, under the Link tab, ensure the correct sheet is selected.
  4. In the dropdown next to it, select Comma-separated values (.csv).
  5. Click the green Publish button and confirm.
  6. Copy the generated URL.
  7. Paste the URL into the input box below and click "Convert".
  8. The JSON will appear in the text box. Click "Copy JSON" and paste it into your zosinfo-data.json file.
